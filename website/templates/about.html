What is House-Tree-Person (HTP) Test?

The House-Tree-Person (HTP) test stands as a time-honored projective psychological assessment tool. Developed to delve into the depths of human cognition, this unique test involves the drawing of three simple yet profound elements: a house, a tree, and a person. Each stroke, line, and nuance in these drawings becomes a window into the complexities of an individual's mind. Psychologists have long used the HTP test to unearth subconscious thoughts, emotions, and traumas.
